Our Facilities

Purpose-built for precision eye care — equipped with the latest diagnostic and surgical technology to serve patients across Bhopal.

Infrastructure

World-Class Facilities in Lalghati

Every room, every instrument, every process is designed to deliver accurate diagnosis and safe, effective treatment.

🔬 Retinal Imaging Suite

Our dedicated retinal suite houses high-resolution fundus cameras and OCT (Optical Coherence Tomography) for detailed retinal mapping and early disease detection.

  • Wide-field fundus photography
  • SD-OCT & Angio-OCT imaging
  • Fluorescein angiography
  • Digital documentation & reporting

⚗️ Surgical Operating Theatre

A fully sterile, NABH-compliant OT equipped for cataract surgery, vitreoretinal procedures, and laser treatments under optimal safety standards.

  • Phacoemulsification system
  • Vitrectomy machine (23G/25G)
  • Surgical microscope with co-axial illumination
  • Laminar airflow ventilation

💡 Laser Treatment Centre

Dedicated laser platform for LASIK refractive correction, retinal photocoagulation, and posterior capsule procedures with micron-level precision.

  • Diode & Argon retinal laser
  • Nd:YAG laser (capsulotomy/iridotomy)
  • SLT for glaucoma management
  • Real-time aiming beam display

📊 Comprehensive Diagnostics

A complete battery of non-invasive tests to assess corneal topography, intraocular pressure, visual fields, and biometry before any surgical intervention.

  • Automated refractometry & keratometry
  • Goldmann applanation tonometry
  • Humphrey visual field analyser
  • IOL Master biometry

🧒 Paediatric Eye Unit

A child-friendly examination space with specialised equipment calibrated for paediatric patients — assessing squint, amblyopia, and refractive errors accurately.

  • Cycloplegic refraction setup
  • Strabismus assessment tools
  • Orthoptic therapy resources
  • Comfortable, calming environment

🏥 Recovery & Consultation Rooms

Post-operative recovery suites with monitoring facilities, plus private consultation rooms for personalised discussions with Dr. Srivastava.

  • Comfortable recovery recliners
  • Vitals monitoring post-surgery
  • Private consultation cabins
  • Clean, accessible OPD layout

Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence

2010

Foundation

Dr. Amit Srivastava established HP Eye & Retina Care in Lalghati with a vision to provide specialised retinal care to the people of Bhopal.

2014

Surgical OT Expansion

Inaugurated a state-of-the-art operating theatre with a phacoemulsification system, enabling in-house cataract and retinal surgery.

2018

Retinal Imaging Suite

Added Angio-OCT and wide-field fundus imaging, elevating diagnostic capability for diabetic retinopathy and macular degeneration.

2023

5,000 Patient Milestone

Crossed 5,000 successfully treated patients, reaffirming HP Eye & Retina Care's position as the trusted eye hospital in central Bhopal.
